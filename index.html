Few of our Projects & Activities

Save Environment:

  • Switch off anything that uses electricity
  • Unplug Devices
  • Use renewable energy sources
  • Trade in your dryer for a good old-fashioned clothesline
  • Run your air conditioner sparingly or not at all
  • Switch to compact florescent or LED light bulbs
  • Get skylights
  • Close doors after you leave a room
  • Don't use electronic exercise machines

Protect from Dengue:

  • Know the enemy: Aedes mosquitoes
  • Know the time dengue mosquitoes bite: in the morning for several hours after daybreak and in the late afternoon for several hours before dark
  • Know which geographies have dengue
  • Avoid getting mosquito bites
  • Protect infants less than 2 months of age by using a carrier draped with mosquito netting with an elastic edge for a tight fit
  • Wear loose, long-sleeved shirts and long pants when outdoors
  • If traveling in dengue infested areas, stay in hotels or resorts that are well screened or air conditioned and take measures to reduce the mosquito population
