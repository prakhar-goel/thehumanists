Welcome to 'The Humanists'

'The Humanists' is a group of Doctors, Engineers and Social Activists who have come together to help the poor and needy sections of the society.

Humanity or 'Insaaniyat' is all about helping people, being humane and lending a helping hand to the underprivileged sections of the society.


Aims and Objectives:


  • To conduct Free Medical Camps (multispeciality, medicine distribution, surgery and diagnostic testing) starting with Bangalore and gradually spreading all over Karnataka and India
  • To conduct awareness programmes on diseases like diabetes, dengue, cancer, etc., during the free medical camps.
  • To conduct programmes to raise awareness about Govt and Pvt welfare schemes/ programmes for needy sections of the society and enable people to get these services.
  • To assist the poor and needy children/families in education related issues like securing admission in schools/colleges, scholarships and to provide school books, stationery etc.,
  • To conduct and participate in 'Green' initiatives like recycling of plastic and other wastes, conservation of water, etc through solid and wet waste management initiatives, tree sapling plantations, water conservation programmes etc.
  • To organize and conduct programmes or drives to raise funds through proper channels to achieve the aims & objectives of The Humanists.
  • To interact/co-ordinate with Govt agencies and other NGO's to facilitate above issues and also be a part of any other scheme/activity which contributes towards the betterment of the underprivileged.
